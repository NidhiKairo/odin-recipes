Pumpkin Pie

Pumpkin pie is a dessert pie with a spiced, pumpkin-based custard filling. The pumpkin and pumpkin pie are both a symbol of harvest time, and pumpkin pie is generally eaten during the fall and early winter.

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin (not pumpkin pie mix)
  • 1 1/4 cups evaporated milk or half and half
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 crust from 1 package (12 oz) frozen Pillsburyâ„¢ Deep Dish Pie Crusts (2 Count)

Important Steps

  1. Mix up crust ingredients
  2. Wrap up and chill crust for 45 minutes
  3. Mix up and cook filling ingredients on stove top
  4. Strain filling
  5. Roll out pie dough, and place in pie pan, chill for 15 minutes
  6. Continue straining filling, if necessary
  7. Cook pie crust for 10 minutes with weights, 5-10 minutes without
  8. Pour filling into crust
  9. Bake at 400 F for 10 minutes, 300 F until done
  10. Cool for approximately 2 hours
  11. Eat
